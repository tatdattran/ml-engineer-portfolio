🏠 House Price Prediction System

Welcome to our House Price Prediction platform. This application estimates residential property prices based on key housing features using a machine learning model (XGBoost) trained on the Ames Housing Dataset from Kaggle.

The model has been optimized to focus on the 9 most influential variables affecting house prices, ensuring both accuracy and usability.

Required Inputs

  • Overall Quality (OverallQual): Overall material and finish quality (scale 1–10)
  • Living Area (GrLivArea): Above-ground living area in square feet (> 0, values in the trained data are between 334 and 5642)
  • Full Bathrooms (FullBath): Number of full bathrooms (1,2,3)
  • Garage Capacity (GarageCars): Number of cars the garage can hold (0,1,2,3,4)
  • Garage Type (GarageType): Attchd (Attached), Detchd (Detached), BuiltIn (Built-in), CarPort, Basment, or 2Types
  • Garage Quality (GarageQual): Ex (Excellent), Gd (Good), TA (Typical/Average), Fa (Fair), Po (Poor), NA (NA)
  • Basement Quality (BsmtQual): Ex, Gd, TA, Fa, (Po, NA)
  • Kitchen Quality (KitchenQual): Ex, Gd, TA, Fa, (Po, NA)
  • Central Air (CentralAir): Y (Yes), N (No)
